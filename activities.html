Our Activities

Discover the diverse range of activities and programs we organize

Education

Free Tutoring Program

Providing free tutoring and mentorship to underprivileged students in mathematics, science, and English. Our volunteers dedicate their time to help students excel in their studies.

50+ Students Helped Weekly Sessions
Education

Scholarship Program

Annual scholarship program for meritorious students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds to support their higher education dreams.

20 Scholarships Annual
Environment

Green Campus Initiative

Large-scale tree plantation drives across the campus and surrounding areas to promote environmental conservation and combat climate change.

500+ Trees Planted Monthly
Environment

Waste Management Program

Comprehensive waste segregation and recycling program to promote sustainable practices and reduce environmental impact.

Campus-wide Ongoing
Health

Blood Donation Camps

Regular blood donation camps in partnership with local hospitals to address blood shortage and save lives in our community.

100+ Units Collected Quarterly
Health

Free Health Checkups

Organizing free health checkup camps for the local community with qualified medical professionals and basic health screenings.

200+ People Screened Bi-annual
Community

Food Distribution Drive

Regular food distribution programs for homeless and underprivileged families, especially during festivals and difficult times.

500+ Meals Served Monthly
Community

Elderly Care Program

Visiting elderly care homes and spending quality time with senior citizens, organizing entertainment programs and providing companionship.

3 Care Homes Weekly
Cultural

Annual Cultural Festival

Grand cultural festival celebrating diversity, featuring traditional dances, music performances, art exhibitions, and cultural competitions.

500+ Participants Annual
Cultural

Talent Showcase

Platform for students to showcase their talents in various fields including singing, dancing, poetry, and other creative arts.

100+ Performers Semester
