Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about joining and participating in our club

How can I join Sairam Leo Club?

To join our club, you can fill out the membership form on our website, attend one of our meetings, or contact us directly. We welcome all students who are passionate about community service and leadership development.

What are the membership requirements?

Membership is open to all students of Sairam Engineering College. We look for individuals who are committed to service, leadership, and making a positive impact in the community.

How often do you meet?

We hold regular meetings twice a month, usually on the first and third Saturday of each month. Special meetings may be called for important events or projects.

What kind of activities do you organize?

We organize a wide range of activities including community service projects, educational programs, environmental initiatives, health camps, cultural events, and leadership development workshops.

Is there a membership fee?

Yes, there is a nominal annual membership fee that helps cover club activities and administrative costs. The fee is kept minimal to ensure accessibility for all students.

Can I volunteer without being a member?

We welcome volunteers for our events and activities. This is also a great way to get to know our club before deciding to become a member.
